A Butler Robot for Restaurant Operation

This project is designed for restaurant operations, specifically automating tasks like food delivery from the kitchen to customers' tables. It manages multiple orders efficiently, ensures accurate navigation in a dynamic environment, and handles scenarios like cancellations and timeouts.

By integrating ROS for task management, path planning, and obstacle avoidance—alongside control systems for trajectory tracking and feedback-based motion regulation—the robot maintains stability and responsiveness in real time. These enhancements streamline restaurant operations, reduce human effort, and improve service efficiency.
